Top 3 Coffee Shops in Duluth, MN

by: Katlyn Dexter

Duluth, Minnesota has many coffee shops that tourists and locals can enjoy year round. These are my recommended
coffee shops to study or stop at when traveling around Duluth. The coffee shops are open year round and offer a
variety of drinks including seasonal drinks. With each coffee shop situated in different locations, grabbing your
drink to go and doing some sightseeing would be a great way to explore Duluth.

  • 190 Coffee and Tea: Situated in the Lincoln Park Neighborhood, 190 Coffee and Tea is a popular place for drinks.
    They are within the Enger Lofts marketplace, so grab your drink of choice and enjoy the adjacent shops.
  • Naturalight Candles + Coffee Bar: Located in Canal Park, Naturalight is a cozy place to grab a coffee before
    enjoying the sights of the lake walk. The coffee bar is in the DeWitt-Seitz marketplace which has plenty of shops
    to stop and look at.
  • At Sara's Table Chester Creek Cafe: Located near the University of Minnesota Duluth campus, the cafe is a great
    place to stop and grab a bite to eat with your drink of choice. Books line the walls so if you need something to do
    while you sip, theres plenty to read.
